This script reads in eBird data for the four target species. It will only work on Bens computer as he has the data.

Our four species are: verdin, black-tailed gnatcatcher, crissal thrasher, and least Bells vireo.

target_common_names <- c("Verdin", "Black-tailed_Gnatcatcher", "Crissal_Thrasher", "Bells_Vireo")
